# Internship Self-Assessment

During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited**, from **May 4, 2025 to July 25, 2025**, I had the opportunity to participate in the **Workforce Bootcamp – First Cloud AI Journey** program. This experience allowed me to apply the knowledge acquired at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology to the development and deployment of a practical system on AWS.

Throughout the internship, I participated in frontend development, AWS architecture design, VPC and data infrastructure deployment, database development, ERD design, website functionality testing, and the preparation of the complete implementation workshop. Through these activities, I improved my skills in web development, cloud architecture design, database administration, system analysis, software testing, technical documentation, and report presentation.

Regarding my working attitude, I always made an effort to complete assigned tasks on schedule, comply with organizational regulations, and proactively research new technologies. I also actively communicated with my supervisor and team members to resolve difficulties, improve the project, and increase overall work efficiency.
